## Getting Started with Tina CMS

Tina CMS provides an excellent editing experience with:

- **Visual editing** - Edit content directly on your site
- **Git-based workflow** - All content is stored in your repository
- **Developer-friendly** - Built for modern web development
- **Flexible schema** - Customize content types to fit your needs
